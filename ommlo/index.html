24 sound presets

Eight categories, each tuned for a different need.

Calm Hands — alpha tones and steady drones
Comfort — brown noise, pink noise, soft textures
Anxiety & Calm — gentle tones and drifts
Sleep — slow, quiet drones and noise blankets
Focus — steady tones for working sessions
Meditation — slow tones for sitting practice
Pain — masking sounds for discomfort
Tinnitus — white and pink noise generators

7 timed programs

Sessions from 5 to 30 minutes that fade in, sustain, and fade out on their own. Press Start and close your eyes.

Journal

Sessions of two minutes or more are logged automatically. A simple three-state check-in lets you track what works for you over time. A 30-day rhythm chart shows your practice at a glance.

Lab Mode

For the curious: a full sound design toolkit. Waveform, frequency, binaural offset, noise mix, envelope, drift, randomness. Design your own patterns and save them.

Headphones recommended

Binaural beats require headphones. The left and right ears receive slightly different frequencies, and your brain perceives the difference as a gentle beat. Other presets work with speakers too.
