Ommlo — Support

Ommlo is a wellness sound app for iOS. It generates quiet tones, binaural beats, and noise textures for relaxation, focus, sleep, meditation, and general wellness.

Ommlo is not a medical device. It is not FDA-approved. It does not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease or condition. If you have a medical condition, consult a healthcare provider. If you have epilepsy or a seizure disorder, consult your doctor before using binaural beat technology. Listen at a comfortable volume. Do not use while driving or operating machinery.
