Ommlo — Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 12, 2026

Ommlo is a therapeutic sound app for iOS, built by Huladyne Labs, LLC. This policy explains what data Ommlo handles and how.

The short version

Ommlo does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything the app stores lives on your device and stays on your device.

What Ommlo stores on your device

  • Preferences: Your settings (Lab Mode toggle, auto-log toggle, post-session check-in toggle, last-played preset, favorites list, which presets you've tried). Stored in iOS UserDefaults on your device.
  • Journal entries: If you have auto-logging enabled, sessions of two minutes or more are recorded locally — the date, preset name, duration, and your optional rating and notes. Stored in a local SwiftData database on your device.
  • Saved patterns: If you create custom sound patterns in Lab Mode, they are saved locally on your device.

What Ommlo does NOT do

  • No account creation or login
  • No data transmitted to any server — ever
  • No cloud sync or backup
  • No analytics, tracking, or telemetry
  • No third-party SDKs or libraries that collect data
  • No advertising or ad identifiers
  • No health data exported to Apple Health or any external system
  • No microphone, camera, location, or contacts access

Data deletion

Deleting the Ommlo app from your device removes all stored data (preferences, journal entries, saved patterns). There is nothing to delete on our end because we never had your data.

Children

Ommlo does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Since Ommlo collects no data from anyone, this is inherently satisfied.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.
