Meet CronDoctor

This started with a telescope. We run a remote rig in Texas that images the sky all night on autopilot, and when a job in that chain fails silently, you lose an entire night of clear sky. We needed something that would tell us what broke and how to fix it, not just that it's down.
Turns out every dev team has the same problem. So we made it for everyone. There's a live demo on the site.

One-Line Setup

One curl call. Works with cron, Lambda, pipelines, K8s, CI/CD. No SDK needed. You'll have monitoring in under a minute.

Performance Drift Detection

It learns what normal looks like for each job and catches slowdowns before they become outages. You don't have to set thresholds.

AI Root Cause Diagnosis

When a job fails, you get a diagnosis, not just a ping. It reads the error, figures out what went wrong, and tells you how to fix it.

Start Monitoring for Free Free forever for up to 5 jobs. Drift detection and AI diagnosis included.
